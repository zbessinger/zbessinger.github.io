About Me

I am a computer vision researcher specializing in multimodal AI systems that combine visual data with geospatial information to extract deeper insights about our world. As a Senior Applied Scientist at Zillow, I develop and implement advanced computer vision and multimodal models that enhance real estate search, personalization, and valuation systems.

I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Kentucky, conducting research under Dr. Nathan Jacobs. My academic foundation includes both master's and bachelor's degrees in Computer Science from Western Kentucky University, where I studied under Dr. Qi Li.
