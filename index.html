About Me

I am a computer vision researcher interested in fusing geospatial data from multiple modalities to better understand the world. I currently work as a Senior Applied Scientist at Zillow where I research, develop, and apply multimodal models for applications in Search and beyond. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from University of Kentucky under the direction of Dr. Nathan Jacobs. I received my master’s and bachelor’s degrees in Computer Science from Western Kentucky University advised by Dr. Qi Li.
